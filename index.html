My Favorite Foods

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps to Make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  1. Gather your ingredients: bread, cheese, and butter.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place one slice, buttered side down, on the skillet.
  5. Top with cheese and second slice, buttered side up.
