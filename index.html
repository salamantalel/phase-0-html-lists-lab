Grilled Cheese Sandwich
A hot sandwich made with melted cheese between slices of toasted bread.

Steps to Make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  1. Gather your ingredients: bread, cheese, and butter.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  4. Place one slice, buttered side down, on the skillet.
  5. Add cheese on top of the bread in the skillet.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top, buttered side up.
  7. Cook until the bottom is golden brown, then flip it.
  8. Cook the other side until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from skillet, slice in half, and serve hot.
